Meet the Founder

Ty Brown

Ty Brown is the founder and lead technician of Essential AV Works. With over 10 years of professional experience in audio engineering, AV systems integration, and technical operations, Ty brings a depth of knowledge that spans live events, broadcast, worship production, and corporate environments.

Essential AV Works was built on the belief that organizations deserve reliable, technically sound AV support without the overhead of a large production company. Whether it's a growing church, a business upgrading its conference room, or a creator building out a studio — every project gets the same level of care, precision, and expertise.
